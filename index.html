About Me



Welcome!


My name is Nidia Carranza. I enjoy anything and everything that involves collaboration and creation! I am a Chicago native, born and raise in the northwest side. I enjoy waking up early and working out when the gym is nearly empty (around 5am). During my free time I love hanging out with my dog, Misterooni, and visiting places I have never been before. My favorite foods include: pepporoni pizza, chiles rellenos, perogis and rice pudding.


When I am not learning and diving into code/web development I teach students in my community in Belmont-Cragin and I run a "Girls Who Code" club. It is my continuous love for learning that drives me to teach others and to always accept all challenges!
